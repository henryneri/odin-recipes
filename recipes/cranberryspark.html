SCL Cooler

Description

SCL Cooler is a refreshing summer drink that consists of mineral water, cranberry juice, and a slice of lime.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 ice
  • 1/2 mineral water
  • 1/2 cranberry juice
  • Slice of lime

Steps

  1. Add 1/4 into a cup
  2. Add 1/2 of mineral water
  3. Add 1/2 of cranberry juice
  4. Add one slice of squeezed lime juice
