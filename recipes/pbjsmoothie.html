PB & J Smoothie

Description

PB & J smoothies are the perfect blend of creamy peanut butter, sweet jelly, and cold, refreshing fruit. They're a delicious and satisfying way to start your day, and make a great snack anytime. If you're in the mood for something sweet and creamy, give a PB & J smoothie a try! You won't be disappointed.

Ingredients

  • 6 Frozen strawberries
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons peanut butter
  • 1 tablespoon blue agave nectar
  • 1 tablespoon flax seeds

Steps

  1. Combine strawberries, milk, peanut butter, blue agave, and flax seeds in a blender and blend until well combined.
